Marketing A/B Testing: Key Insights & Findings

This A/B Testing dashboard provides insights into ad performance and user engagement, helping to measure the effectiveness of advertisements (Ads) versus public service announcements (PSAs).

1️⃣ Conversion Performance

  • Total of 14.84K conversions, with Ads converting at a 3% rate and PSAs at 2%.
  • The majority of conversions came from the Ad group, indicating higher effectiveness.

2️⃣ User Engagement

  • 588.10K total users, with Ads engaging 564.58K users compared to 23.52K for PSAs.
  • Users exposed to Ads interacted more frequently than those shown PSAs.

3️⃣ Advertisement Reach & Frequency

  • Most Ads were displayed on Fridays, showing peak Ad engagement.
  • The most Ads seen by a single user was 2.065K for Ads and 907 for PSAs, highlighting strong Ad visibility.

4️⃣ Time-Based Trends

  • The heatmap analysis revealed that Ads had the highest views between 10 AM - 6 PM.
  • Thursdays and Fridays generated the highest conversions, making them optimal Ad days.

5️⃣ Actionable Recommendations

  • Increase ad budget on peak engagement days (Thursday & Friday) for maximum ROI.
  • Optimize ad placement during high-engagement hours (10 AM - 6 PM) to reach more users.
  • Enhance PSA engagement strategies to improve user interaction.

This analysis helps marketers refine their ad strategies, improve user engagement, and optimize conversion rates for better campaign effectiveness.
