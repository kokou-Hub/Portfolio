The Story Behing the Numbers

1. Spotlight on Sales Success

This First dashboard explores sales and performance for a home and business security provider during October 2023.
Below are key highlights:

    A Month of Strong Performance

    The sales team delivered an impressive 59,000 jobs sold during the month, with the Southeast Gulf Region leading the charge as the top-performing area. Customers consistently chose Control Tier 3, making it the best-selling product, while the Direct-Phone channel proved to be the most effective sales driver, accounting for over 60% of total sales.

    Customer and Product Trends

    The data reveals that NEW_SALE orders dominated, far surpassing other types like ADDON and REACTIVATION. This indicates a strong ability to attract new customers. However, complementary products, such as Burglar Alarm, also saw significant traction, suggesting opportunities for bundled offerings to maximize revenue.

    Regional Dynamics

    The Southeast Gulf Region stands out for its remarkable performance, while other regions like Mid-Atlantic and Northeast also contributed meaningfully. There is an opportunity to replicate the strategies used in the Southeast Gulf Region to boost sales in underperforming areas.

    Channel Success

    While the Direct-Phone channel leads sales efforts, channels like E-Commerce and Direct-Field represent untapped potential. Optimizing and strengthening these channels could drive incremental growth and diversify revenue streams.

    Daily Trends

    Sales data shows clear peaks and dips in activity throughout the month. Identifying the factors contributing to these fluctuations could help focus marketing and promotional efforts on low-performing days, creating a more consistent sales pattern.

    2. Addressing Cancellation Challenges

    This second dashboard explores cancellations and their impact for a home and business security provider during October 2023.
    Below are key highlights:

      During the month, the organization recorded 9,942 job cancellations, resulting in a significant revenue loss of $6.63 million, which equates to 25.76% of potential revenue. The GA Atlanta region stands out as having the highest number of cancellations, signaling critical areas that need immediate attention.

      Daily Cancellation Trends

      Cancellations fluctuated throughout the month, with certain days showing clear spikes. This presents an opportunity to investigate the underlying causes, such as service delays, unmet expectations, or regional challenges, to minimize cancellations on peak days.

      Channel Dynamics

      While the Direct-Phone channel drives the majority of sales, it also contributes significantly to cancellations. Optimizing the post-sale engagement process for this channel, such as better follow-up or customer education, could mitigate losses.

      Product Cancellation Insights

      Similar to its dominance in sales, Control Tier 3 also leads in cancellations. This trend suggests potential issues with onboarding, installation, or customer expectations. Addressing these gaps is essential to reducing churn for this high-performing product.

      Regional Focus

      In addition to GA Atlanta, other regions like Northeast and Southeast Gulf also recorded notable cancellations. Identifying and replicating best practices from lower-cancellation regions can help address these trends.

      Opportunities to Mitigate Cancellations

      • Focus on High-Cancellation Regions: Address customer complaints and operational inefficiencies in GA Atlanta.
      • Strengthen Post-Sale Support: Implement proactive customer engagement and follow-up processes.
      • Improve Product Experience: Align customer expectations with product capabilities, and enhance the installation experience for Control Tier 3.
      • Leverage Data Insights: Use trends from daily cancellations to plan targeted interventions.
